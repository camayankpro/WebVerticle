How much does a professional firm website cost from WebVerticle?
+
₹11,000 one-time. That's it. No monthly hosting fees. No annual maintenance charges. No hidden costs for content or SEO. The only separate expense is your domain name (approximately ₹800–₹1,200 per year), which you register yourself. Everything else — hosting, SSL, professional email, articles, SEO setup — is included in the ₹11,000.
Are there any monthly fees or renewal charges?
+
No monthly fees at all. Hosting is free forever via GitHub Pages. SSL renews automatically at no cost. Professional email via Zoho Mail is free. The only thing that renews annually is your domain name (~₹800–₹1,200/year), which is a direct payment to the domain registrar — not to us.
How long does it take to get my website live?
+
48 hours after payment and receiving your details. We need four things from you: your firm name, city, phone number, and address. Once we have those and payment is confirmed, we handle everything — domain configuration, hosting setup, content personalisation, SEO setup, email configuration, and WhatsApp button. You get a live, working website in 48 hours.
Do I need to provide content or write anything?
+
No. You provide four basic details (firm name, city, phone, address) and choose a colour theme. All the content — service pages, SEO articles, about section, contact section, everything — is pre-written and included. If you have specific content you'd like added (awards, team members, specific services), we can incorporate that too.
Will my website actually rank on Google?
+
We build every website with the correct foundation for local SEO — city-specific targeting, proper heading structure, keyword-optimised service pages, schema markup, fast loading speed, and mobile optimisation. Ranking depends on competition in your city and how long the site has been live. Most clients see meaningful local search visibility within 2–4 months of going live. We give you the best possible start — the rest depends on search engine timelines.
How is WebVerticle different from a generic web designer?
+
A generic designer builds websites. WebVerticle builds websites for specific professions. The difference is the content — every article, every service page, every line of copy is written by someone who deeply understands your profession, your clients, and what those clients search for. A generic designer will give you a template. We give you a domain-specific digital identity that sounds like it was built by someone who knows your work.
